# Prompt Gallery: Content Categories

This document outlines the official content categories for the Prompt Gallery app.

## 1. Portrait Angles (Specialty Category)

A collection focused on capturing portraits from various professional angles.

-   **Eye-Level Portrait:** Straight-on, creating a personal and direct connection.
-   **High-Angle Portrait (From Above):** Shot from above, often making the subject appear more vulnerable or smaller.
-   **Low-Angle Portrait (From Below):** Shot from below, making the subject appear powerful, tall, or dominant.
-   **Side-Profile Portrait (Left/Right):** Captures the subject's profile, highlighting their facial contours.
-   **Three-Quarter Portrait:** A view between a frontal and a side view, offering depth and dimension.
-   **Over-the-Shoulder Portrait:** Shot from behind the subject's shoulder, creating a sense of intimacy or a candid moment.
-   **Dutch Angle Portrait:** The camera is tilted, creating a sense of unease, disorientation, or dynamic energy.
-   **Close-Up Portrait (Extreme Detail):** Tightly framed on the subject's face, focusing on emotions and details.

## 2. Artistic Styles

-   **Hyperrealistic:** Incredibly detailed and lifelike images.
-   **Anime & Manga:** Inspired by Japanese animation and comics.
-   **Cinematic:** Evokes the feeling of a movie still, with dramatic lighting and composition.
-   **Fantasy & Mystical:** For creating magical worlds, creatures, and characters.
-   **Sci-Fi & Futuristic:** Cyberpunk cities, futuristic technology, and space exploration.
-   **Abstract & Surreal:** Non-representational, dreamlike, and bizarre visuals.
-   **Vintage & Retro:** Inspired by aesthetics from past decades (e.g., 80s synthwave, 50s film noir).
-   **Minimalist:** Clean compositions, simple subjects, and negative space.
-   **Watercolor & Painted:** Simulates traditional art mediums.

## 3. Themes & Concepts

-   **Landscapes & Scenery:** From majestic mountains to serene beaches.
-   **Architecture & Structures:** Buildings, interiors, and cityscapes.
-   **Nature & Wildlife:** Animals, plants, and natural phenomena.
-   **Food & Drink:** Deliciously styled culinary creations.
-   **Vehicles & Machinery:** Cars, spaceships, and intricate machines.
-   **Fashion & Style:** High-fashion portraits and stylistic concepts.

## 4. Technical & Compositional

-   **Macro Photography:** Extreme close-up shots of tiny subjects.
-   **Long Exposure:** Capturing motion blur, light trails, and dreamy water.
-   **Golden Hour:** Soft, warm, and dimensional lighting just after sunrise or before sunset.
-   **Blue Hour:** The period just before sunrise or after sunset with deep blue light.
-   **Neon & Noir:** Dark scenes punctuated by vibrant neon lights.
